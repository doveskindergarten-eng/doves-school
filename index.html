About DOVES

At DOVES, we believe every African child deserves quality education. Our mixed-race community of teachers and pupils learn together through practical skills, academics, and play.

Our Vision

"The Sky Is The Limit"

To be the leading primary school in Serere District that raises confident, skilled, and God-fearing leaders who transform their communities through knowledge, character, and practical innovation.

Our Mission

Ensuring the social - economic attitude and promotion of life, sustainability, knowledgeable and beneficial citizenery focusing factors affecting communities by:

  • Delivering education curriculum with excellence
  • Integrating agriculture, ICT, and life skills
  • Nurturing discipline, creativity, and patriotism
  • Maintaining a safe, caring school environment

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in academics and character.

Inclusivity

Every African child is welcome, valued, and supported.

Practical Skills

We teach children to work with both head and hands.

Integrity

We raise honest, responsible future leaders.
